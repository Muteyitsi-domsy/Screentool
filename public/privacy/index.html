Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

Data Collection

We collect minimal data: your email address (used for authentication and licensing, stored by Supabase), payment details (processed securely by Paddle — we never see your card details), and basic technical logs. We do not track the content of your screenshots.

Local-First Architecture

ScreenFrame is built on a local-first architecture. Your images and projects are stored in your browser's IndexedDB. We never see your assets unless you explicitly choose to sync to our optional cloud backup (Studio/Enterprise only).

Usage & Cookies

We use essential cookies and browser storage to maintain your authentication session (managed by Supabase) and licensing status. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — User authentication and account data storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Paddle — Payment processing and subscription management (Merchant of Record). Paddle Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — Application hosting

User Rights

Under GDPR guidelines, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@screenframe.app.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
